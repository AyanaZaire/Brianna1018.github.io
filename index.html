About



My idea is a skin and hair care improvement website, in which I provide tips and tricks on how to prevent damage and maintain the health of your skin and hair. I will also provide products to help soothe the needs of my customers and followers. I aim to help guide women with health tips and tricks to help improve their hair or skin care situations, like if they have acne or damaged hair.I want this website to help women who have problems with their skin and/or their hair so that over time they can have more confidence and lead to a healthier appearance and lifestyle. The tagline of my website is “When you think Beauty, Think Us!” This idea came to me because this is what I currently struggle with at the moment and it would be nice to also help give people tips on how to improve their situation if they are in the same position.
